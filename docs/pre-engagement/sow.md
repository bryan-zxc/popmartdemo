# Statement of Work — Global Retail Sales Analysis

Contoso Electronics has engaged us to analyse their global sales data (2015–2021) to support two strategic initiatives.

## 1. Product Line Rationalisation

Identify the most and least profitable product lines. The board wants to prune underperforming products to free up capital and focus investment on high-margin categories.

**Deliverable:** Profitability analysis by product and category with a recommended shortlist for discontinuation.

## 2. Store Performance and Best Practice

Identify the best and worst performing stores. For top performers, understand what drives their success (product mix, location demographics, store size efficiency) so those practices can be replicated. For the worst performers, assess whether converting to online-only fulfilment would reduce costs while maintaining revenue.

**Deliverable:** Store performance benchmarking with actionable recommendations for underperformers.
